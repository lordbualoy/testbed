Strength
Description
A measure of how physically strong a creature is
Effects
  • Increases Carrying Capacity
  • Increases Grappling Power
  • Increases Muscle Powered Attack Damage
  • Increases the range of Muscle Powered Ranged Weapons (Bow, Sling, Throwing)
  • Increases Blocking
  • Increases Mass
  • Increases Aquatic Speed
  • Decreases Buoyancy
Intelligence
Description
A measure of a creature's problem-solving, decision-making ability, and how quickly it learns
Effects
  • Increases all Damage
  • Increases Grappling Power
  • Increases Throwing Precision
  • Increases Shooting Precision
  • Decreases the chance of getting Bluffed by another character
Constitution
Description
A measure of how sturdy a creature is
Effects
  • Increases Maximum Health Point
  • Increases Immune System efficiency
Willpower
Description
A measure of a creature’s strength of mind as in ability to withstand psychological stress
Effects
  • Decreases the chance of getting Intimidated, Brainwashed, Charmed, Seduced, Hypnotized by another character
  • Decreases the chance of getting succumb to fear
  • Increases the chance of resisting Interrogation and Torture
Agility
Description
A measure of how agile a creature is
Reflex
Description
A measure of how quickly can a creature subconsciously responds to external stimuluses such as attacks
Effects
  • Increases Parrying
Speed of Thought
Description
A measure of a creature's thinking speed
Effects
  • Increase conscious response speed to all external stimuluses
  • Increase arithmetic operations per second
Endurance
Description
A measure of how much energy a creature can exerted before getting exhausted
Effects
  • Increases Maximum Stamina Point
  • Increases Carrying Capacity
  • Determine how long can a character keep a bow string drawn
Dexterity
Description
A measure of a creature coarse and fine motor skills
Effects
  • Increases Grappling Power
  • Increases Throwing Precision
  • Increases Shooting Precision
  • Increases Parrying
Lacertilia Index
Description
A measure of a creature's affinity with the Lacertilia
Effects
  • Minimum Lacertilia Index required for spellcasting is at 120
Luck
Description
A measure of a creature's luck
Effects
  • Increases chance of success on all rolls
  • Increases chance of winning in gambling
