Description

Lacertilia is a cosmic energy that moves around the universe like the wind but travel at the speed of light.Lacertilia were dangerous to life but fortunately most of the Lacertilia in space got blocked by Nabillon magnetic field and only a tiny amount made it to Nabillon's surface and travel around the world and eventually bounced back into space again.This little amount of Lacertilia that made it to the surface was harmless to organic life but its presence has infused the matters on Nabillon be it organic or inorganic; this Lacertilia residue that remained within organisms and the atmosphere has allowed them to tap into and perform magic by manipulating this cosmic energy.However if the magnetic field of Nabillon were to become weaker and Lacertilia that managed to reach the surface were increased to something like a thousand fold it will cause a planet wide extinction

Due to the presence of Lacertilia, the night sky of Nabillon appears a lot more bright and colorful than the nightsky of earth
