Nabillon is a medieval fantasy world, Nabillon was a large, fertile world dominated by vast plains, dense forests, and lush swamps. Besides the human and the elves it was originally home to many ancient races, including the angels, demons and the dragon.

Lacertilia

Main article: Lacertilia
The most defining feature of Nabillon is the existent of Lacertilia. Lacertilia was a cosmic energy native to this universe. Nabillon was constantly being bombarded with Lacertilia but most of it couldn't reach Nabillon surface due to Nabillon's magnetic field nevertheless the presence of Lacertilia that do made it to Nabillon surface has given Nabillan ability to use magic
